Build discipline, awareness, and efficiency with DOLPTIPID!

Doltipid is a budget monitoring website, designed to help Senior High School students manage and track their weekly expenses in food, transportation, and savings.

Optional Tips

Budget Rule: 60 / 30 / 10

Use the 60-30-10 rule to manage your money wisely.

60% - Needs

Essentials: Bills, Rent, Groceries, Transport

30% - Wants

Leisure: Food, Shopping, Subscriptions

10% - Savings

Emergency Fund, Investments, or Goals

Saving Tips

  • Track your spending weekly so you can see where your money goes.
  • Pay yourself first.
  • Set a weekly spending limit.
  • Build an emergency fund.
  • Use the 24-hour rule.
